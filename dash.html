What is Zoho CRM? Zoho CRM is an award winning CRM which is trusted by a global network of over 250,000 businesses in 180 countries to convert more leads, engage with customers, and grow their revenue. Zoho CRM acts as a single repository to bring your sales, marketing, and customer support activities together, and streamline your process, policy, and people in one platform.
